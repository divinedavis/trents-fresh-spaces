A fresh coat of interior paint is the fastest, most affordable way to transform a room. At Trent's Fresh Spaces, we handle every interior painting project across York County with careful preparation, premium materials and a finish that lasts.

What our interior painting includes

Every interior job is different, but the care behind it never changes. We typically cover:

  • Interior walls — bedrooms, living rooms, hallways, dining rooms and more, in the exact color you choose.
  • Ceilings — bright, even ceilings that make a whole room feel taller and cleaner.
  • Trim, baseboards & doors — crisp, professional lines where walls meet woodwork.
  • Accent walls — a single bold wall to add depth and personality.
  • Drywall prep & repair — patching, sanding and priming so the finish looks flawless.
  • Whole-home refreshes — top-to-bottom interior repaints, room by room, on a clear schedule.

Our interior painting process

1. Free on-site estimate

We walk your space, talk through colors and finishes, and give you a clear, written quote up front — no pressure and no surprises.

2. Protect & prep

Furniture is moved or covered, floors and fixtures are protected, and surfaces are cleaned, patched, sanded and primed. Good prep is the difference between paint that looks fine and paint that looks flawless for years.

3. Paint with care

We cut clean edges by hand, roll smooth even coats, and apply as many coats as the color needs for full, consistent coverage.

4. Walk-through & clean-up

We tidy up completely and walk the finished space with you. We're not done until you're thrilled with how your fresh space looks.
