Trent's Fresh Spaces · Setup

Connect your calendar

One-time setup so online bookings appear on your calendar — and your own calendar blocks hide booked times on the site. Takes ~3 minutes.

1 Email for confirmations & invites

This lets the site email a calendar invite + confirmation when someone books. It uses a Gmail App Password — not your normal password and not any Google API.
Get one: myaccount.google.com/apppasswords (needs 2-Step Verification on). Create one named "Fresh Spaces", copy the 16 characters.

2 Your Google Calendar (read-only)

So events you add to your own Google Calendar block those times on the site. On a computer: Google Calendar → hover your calendar → ⋮ → Settings and sharing → scroll to "Secret address in iCal format" → copy the link (ends in .ics).
Tip: when you add your own appointments on your iPhone, save them to your Gmail calendar (Settings → Apps → Calendar → Default Calendar) so they hide times on the site.

Optional — skip if you use Google Apple / iCloud Calendar

Most people can skip this. If your Google Calendar (Step 2) is the one you actually use — even if it just shows up in the Apple Calendar app on your iPhone — you're already covered and can leave this blank.
Only fill this in if you keep some appointments in a separate iCloud calendar. You'll need an app-specific password (not your normal Apple password, not any API): account.apple.com → Sign-In & Security → App-Specific Passwords → create one named "Fresh Spaces".
